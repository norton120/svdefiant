_Defiant_ is a 1989 Bayfield 36, a classic Canadian-built blue water cruiser. This site documents her refit and restoration, and will eventually serve as a public voyage log as she returns to the water.

We have lived aboard _Defiant_ since her purchase in September of 2025, working to make her equal parts seaworthy and a worthy home. 

## The Crew
**Myself:** Captain by chair only, with intentions to solo sail _Defiant_ up and down the eastern seaboard beginning this year. 

**Bash the Dog:** Expert at finding stable places to sleep while underway. 

This is our journey.
